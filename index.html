April 2026 — Proposed Budget Plan: $20K SP + $10K DSP
🎯
The Strategy Shift: Efficiency → Growth
March proved you can do more with less on Sponsored Products. April's plan locks in that efficiency while adding DSP as a retargeting layer to capture the 65% of revenue that is currently organic — people who find you naturally but may not return. The goal: keep SP tight and efficient, use DSP to convert browsers into buyers and buyers into repeat customers.

📡 DSP (Display) — $10,000
New channel · Reaches buyers before & after search · Different game than SP
⚠ Important: DSP ≠ Sponsored Products
DSP ACoS will look worse than SP (typically 50–150%) because it's upper-funnel. Measure it by new-to-brand % and repeat purchase rate, not ACoS alone. Give it 6–8 weeks before judging.
🔁 Retargeting — Product Page Visitors $5,000 · 50%
People who viewed your listings but didn't buy. Highest intent, lowest cost to convert. Your best DSP investment.
🔄 Win-Back — Past Purchasers $3,000 · 30%
Re-engage customers who bought 60–90+ days ago. Drive Subscribe & Save sign-ups. Lifetime value play — supplements are a repeat purchase category.
⚔ Competitor Product Pages $2,000 · 20%
Show your ads on competitor detail pages. Intercept customers comparing options. Works best for HNS vs. direct supplement competitors.
